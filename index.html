Design of A CMOS Rectifier Chip For Ambient RF Energy Harvesting

Introduction: This project aims at studying and exploring the performance of different CMOS rectifiers under the condition that their input power is extremely low.

Techniques used: CMOS circuit and layout design, IBM130nm microfabrication technology, PCB design, wire-bonding...

Publication: Wang, Y.; Sawan, M., "High-efficiency CMOS rectifier dedicated for multi-band ambient RF energy harvesting," Electronics, Circuits and Systems (ICECS), 2014 21st IEEE International Conference on , vol., no., pp.179,182, 7-10 Dec. 2014.
